Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

SM Tech Lab ("we", "us", or "our") operates the OpenPen AI mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data

When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and Google account ID to create and manage your account.

Voice Recordings

When you create a voice note, your audio recording is sent to the Groq API for transcription. Audio recordings are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers or by Groq.

Transcribed Text / Notes

Your transcribed notes are stored locally on your device. We do not store your notes on our servers.

Usage Data

We collect usage information including:

  • Number of transcriptions performed
  • Audio recording duration
  • Subscription status and trial usage

Device Information

We may collect device identifiers to manage your account and provide the service.

Preferences

We store your app preferences such as theme settings (dark/light mode) to personalize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process voice recordings into text transcriptions
  • Manage your account and subscription status
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Communicate with you about service updates

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

Supabase

We use Supabase for user authentication and subscription management. Your account and subscription data are stored in Supabase's secure infrastructure. Supabase's privacy policy applies to data stored on their platform.

Groq API

We use Groq's API for audio transcription and text enhancement. Your audio recordings are sent to Groq for processing. Please refer to Groq's privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Google

We use Google OAuth for sign-in authentication. When you sign in with Google, Google's privacy policy governs the data shared during authentication.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication is managed with secure JWT tokens
  • Account data is protected by Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by following these steps:

How to Delete Your Account

  • Step 1: Open the OpenPen AI app on your device
  • Step 2: Tap the Settings icon (gear icon) in the sidebar
  • Step 3: Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  • Step 4: Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted from our servers:

  • Your account profile and email address
  • Your device identifier
  • Your subscription and billing information
  • Your usage tracking records (transcription history, credit usage)

What Data is NOT Affected

  • Notes stored locally on your device will remain on your device. You can manually delete them from the app or by uninstalling the app.

Important Information

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • All data is deleted immediately upon confirmation
  • If you have an active subscription, it will be cancelled
  • You can create a new account after deletion, but your previous data cannot be recovered

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you are unable to access the app or need assistance with account deletion, please contact us at support.smtechlab@gmail.com with the email address associated with your account, and we will process your deletion request within 7 business days.

6. Data Deletion Without Account Deletion

You can request deletion of specific data without deleting your entire account. Here is how you can manage your data:

Data You Can Delete Directly

  • Notes: Swipe left on any note in the app and tap "Delete" to remove it from your device
  • All Notes: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored notes

Data You Can Request Us to Delete

To request deletion of your server-side data without deleting your account, email us at support.smtechlab@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and specify which data you want deleted:

  • Usage tracking records (transcription history and credit usage logs)
  • Device identifier

Data That Cannot Be Deleted Without Account Deletion

  • Email address and account profile — required to maintain your account
  • Subscription information — required for billing and service access

We will process data deletion requests within 7 business days and confirm completion via email.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support.smtechlab@gmail.com.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the App
  • Delete your account (see Section 5 for detailed steps)
  • Request deletion of specific data without deleting your account (see Section 6)
  • Request information about what data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support.smtechlab@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
